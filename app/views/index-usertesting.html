A list of all user testing dates and locations and what was tested

Doncaster Jobcentre - 14/08/19

  • To test the assisted digital screen and changes to the address input screen

Holbeck Elderly Aid - 29/08/19

  • Explore attitudes to borrowing within the pension credit claimant group, and digital abilities, in order to gain a broader understanding of why so few pension credit customers apply for budgeting loans
  • Explore levels of digital ability in order to feed into an assisted digital strategy for the project

Telephone interviews - 09/09/19

  • Explore why some pension credit customers chose to complete a clerical application form rather than apply online.
  • To discover whether these customers would use an online application in the future

Hull Jobcentre - 26/09/19

  • To test the end to end prototype, including postcode lookup and summary screen

Fiveways House, Birmingham - 02/10/19

  • Processor observation/interview
  • Team meeting with work coaches, and 1-1 interview with an enquiry team member
  • To identify problems currently experienced by processors in respect of processing ABL forms that have been rejected as robotic exceptions.

Chesterfield Jobcentre - 10/10/19

  • To identify work coach actions when a customer requests a clerical form to apply for a budgeting loan
  • understand whether the online service is actively promoted

Chesterfield Jobcentre - 21/10/19

  • 1-1 Interview with a visiting officer
  • Explore the demand for visiting officers to provide support to customers to complete budgeting loan applications, and understand how changes to the offer and acceptance process might impact their working processes

Skegness Service Centre - 30/10/19

  • Processor observation/interview
  • To identify problems currently experienced by processors in respect of processing ABL forms that have been rejected as robotic exceptions

Hull Jobcentre - 04/11/19

  • Pop up research
  • To further understand the pain points caused to the customer of the current offer letter
